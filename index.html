Music Box

Build a music box with buzzer of microduino kit. The sample song is "little star".

Night Lamp

A color LED light contolled by light sensor and sound sensor, which detect surrounding environment. If the environment is dark enough and there is some noise made, the LED will turn on automatically.

Light Alarm

An alarm detect the strength of light environment. It changes color according to the changes of strength of light. When the environment becomes too dark, the alarm will ring to warn users.

Night Owl

An animal active in the night. It will roll its red shining eyes when someone passes it in the night.
