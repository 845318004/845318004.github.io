Night Lamp

A color LED light contolled by light sensor and sound sensor, which detect surrounding environment. If the environment is dark enough and there is some noise made, the LED will turn on automatically.

Tools

core(328p16M,5V), USB TTL, Sensor Hub, Sensor Cable, USB cable, Color LED, Light Sensor, Microphone Sensor (all from microduino kit)

Step 1: Prepare all modules, put them together according to instructions.

Step 2: Download and input codes of Night Lamp in arduino.

Step 3: Click "verify" to check.

Step 4: Click "Upload", LED will turn on automatically when light environment and noise level suit the condition.
